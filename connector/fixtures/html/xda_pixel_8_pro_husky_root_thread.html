xda_member_a Feb 10, 2024
Pixel 8 Pro husky on Android 14 build AP1A.240205.002 can be rooted by patching init_boot.img with Magisk, then flashing the patched image from the matching factory image. Bootloader must be unlocked first. 
fastboot flash init_boot_a patched_init_boot.img
xda_member_b Apr 18, 2024
Warning for Pixel 8 Pro husky: do not flash vendor_boot.img or init_boot.img from Android 14 on Android 15 beta. Mismatched images caused bootloop and data loss for several users. Use the same build package and keep a rollback path.
